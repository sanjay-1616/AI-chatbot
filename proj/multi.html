BSc in Multimedia & Animation – Bring Ideas to Life

A BSc in Multimedia & Animation is a 3-year undergraduate program that focuses on the creation of digital content such as animation, visual effects, and interactive multimedia. It prepares students for dynamic careers in creative digital industries.

Key Aspects

  • Duration: 3 years
  • Eligibility: 10+2 pass from a recognized board
  • Focus: Animation, VFX, Digital Media, Graphic Design

Curriculum Overview

  • 2D & 3D Animation Techniques
  • Visual Effects (VFX)
  • Digital Filmmaking & Image Processing
  • Multimedia Production

Skills You’ll Learn

  • Animation: Principles, storytelling, and character design
  • Visual Effects: Techniques for film, TV, and games
  • Graphic Design: Creating visuals for print, web, and apps
  • Multimedia: Combining video, sound, and animation

Software Tools Covered

  • Autodesk Maya
  • Adobe Photoshop & After Effects
  • Compositing Tools
  • Web Design Software

Creative & Technical Development

  • Visual communication principles
  • Illustration, composition, and perspective
  • Creative thinking through real-world projects
  • Problem-solving with digital tools

Career Opportunities

  • Animator: Designing animated sequences
  • VFX Artist: Creating special effects for media
  • Game Developer: Building engaging interactive games
  • Web Designer: Designing functional and attractive websites
  • Graphic Designer: Crafting visual content for various platforms
  • Storyboard Artist / Character Designer / Compositor

Salary and Scope

  • Entry-Level: ₹3,00,000 – ₹5,00,000 per annum
  • Mid-Level (3-5 yrs): ₹5,00,000 – ₹8,00,000 per annum
  • Freelance: ₹500 – ₹5,000+ per hour (based on skill/project)

Conclusion

A BSc in Multimedia & Animation provides a perfect blend of creativity and technology. From graphic design to immersive VFX and animation, this degree opens doors to various exciting digital careers in the media and entertainment industry.
