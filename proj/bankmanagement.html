B.Com (Bank Management)

B.Com (Bank Management) is a 3-year undergraduate degree focused on the banking and financial services industry. It equips students with comprehensive knowledge in finance, risk management, and banking operations, preparing them for professional roles in the banking sector.

Key Aspects of the Program

  • Duration: 3 years (6 semesters)
  • Focus: Banking operations, customer service, and financial management

Curriculum

  • Accounting and Finance
  • Banking Theory and Practice
  • Financial Markets
  • Risk Management
  • Investment Analysis
  • Customer Service
  • Banking Technology

Career Opportunities

  • Bank Manager
  • Loan Officer
  • Compliance Officer
  • Credit Analyst
  • Investment Analyst
  • Financial Analyst
  • Human Resource Manager in banking/financial institutions

Scope

  • Provides expertise in banking operations and risk management
  • Enhances understanding of financial markets and investment strategy
  • Opens diverse opportunities in both public and private sector banks

Higher Study Options

  • M.Com (Bank Management)
  • MBA (Finance/Banking)
  • Financial Risk Management (FRM)
  • Business Accounting and Taxation (BAT)
  • CA, CS, ICMA and other professional certifications

Eligibility

  • Completion of 10+2 or equivalent with Commerce stream
  • Preferred subjects: Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics

Colleges Offering This Program

  • Hindustan College of Arts & Science
  • Anna Adarsh College for Women
  • And other recognized institutions across India

B.Com (Bank Management) is ideal for students looking to pursue a career in finance and banking with options for growth into management roles, professional certifications, or higher education.
