Bachelor of Public Health (BPH)

The Bachelor of Public Health (BPH) is a 3 to 4-year undergraduate program designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills to improve community and population health. It emphasizes preventive healthcare, public health policy, and effective health communication.

Program Highlights

  • Duration: 3–4 years
  • Eligibility: 10+2 with 50% minimum marks in PCB (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • Focus Areas: Epidemiology, Biostatistics, Environmental Health, Health Policy, and Health Promotion

Why Study Public Health?

  • Impact: Contribute to disease prevention and health promotion
  • Growing Demand: Increasing career opportunities in India and globally
  • Versatility: Roles in research, policy-making, consulting, education, and administration

Scope of Public Health

  • Diverse Career Paths: Healthcare consultants, public health researchers, environmental health officers
  • Government and NGO Work: Roles in national and international health agencies
  • Research and Policy: Conduct studies and help shape public health policies
  • Global Health: Address global health challenges across borders

Skills You’ll Gain

  • Analytical and critical thinking
  • Effective communication and public engagement
  • Leadership and project management
  • Data collection and statistical analysis

Key Roles and Responsibilities

  • Healthcare Consultants: Improve healthcare systems and operations
  • Public Health Researchers: Study disease trends, prevention, and control strategies
  • Environmental Health Officers: Ensure safe and healthy environments
  • Health Educators: Promote healthy habits and community wellness
  • Policy Analysts: Create health-related policies and reform strategies

Career and Salary Prospects

  • Entry-Level Salary: ₹3.60 – ₹5 LPA
  • Average Salary: ₹5 – ₹7 LPA
  • Experienced Professionals: ₹6 – ₹12 LPA
  • Top Employers: WHO, UNICEF, Ministry of Health, NGOs, Healthcare Startups, Hospitals
  • Factors Affecting Salary: Location, specialization, employer type, experience
