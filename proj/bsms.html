BSMS - Siddha Medicine Career Path

The Bachelor of Siddha Medicine and Surgery (BSMS) is a 5.5-year undergraduate program that focuses on Siddha medicine—an ancient Indian healing system rooted in holistic wellness and traditional practices. Upon completion, graduates can use the title "Dr." and work in various healthcare sectors.

What is BSMS?

BSMS falls under the AYUSH systems (Ayurveda, Yoga, Naturopathy, Unani, Siddha, Homeopathy). It emphasizes natural healing, diagnostic skills, and therapeutic practices based on Siddha philosophy.

Program Duration

  • Total Duration: 5.5 years
  • Academic Study: 4.5 years
  • Internship: 1 year of mandatory hands-on training

Eligibility & Admission

  • 10+2 or equivalent with Physics, Chemistry, Biology (minimum 50%)
  • NEET-UG qualification is typically mandatory
  • Some institutions may require Botany/Zoology knowledge

Course Curriculum

  • Siddha principles and diagnostics
  • Human Anatomy & Physiology
  • Biochemistry and Microbiology
  • Tamil language and Communicative English
  • Seven elements of Siddha: Plasma, Blood, Muscle, Fat, Bone, Nerve, Semen

Why Choose BSMS?

  • Comprehensive understanding of holistic and natural treatments
  • Eligibility to practice as a registered Siddha medical practitioner
  • Strong career potential in both public and private sectors

Career Opportunities

  • Clinical Practice: Government/private hospitals or personal clinics
  • Research & Development: Work with herbal drug companies and wellness brands
  • Teaching: Educator or lecturer in Siddha medical colleges
  • Drug Manufacturing: Join Siddha pharmaceutical or herbal product units
  • Health & Wellness Centers: Therapy, detox, Panchakarma support roles

Salary Range

  • Entry-Level: ₹2.3 LPA – ₹3.5 LPA
  • Mid-Level: ₹4 LPA – ₹6 LPA
  • Senior/Private Practice: ₹7 LPA – ₹12 LPA and above

Salaries vary by location, experience, specialization, and certifications. Urban centers often offer higher pay.

Postgraduate Options

  • MD (Siddha)
  • PhD in Siddha Pharmacology or Clinical Research
  • MBA in Hospital Management

Top Institutions Offering BSMS

  • Government Siddha Medical College, Chennai
  • National Institute of Siddha, Tambaram
  • Govt Siddha Medical College, Palayamkottai
  • Anna University affiliated Siddha institutions
