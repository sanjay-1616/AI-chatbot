B.Sc. in Perfusion Technology

A B.Sc. in Perfusion Technology is a 3-year undergraduate program that prepares individuals to become skilled cardiac perfusionists. These professionals operate heart-lung machines during cardiac surgeries, playing a vital role in maintaining patients' cardiopulmonary function during critical procedures.

Key Highlights

  • Duration: 3 years
  • Eligibility: 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology (minimum 50%)
  • Course Fee: ₹4,800 to ₹6 Lakhs (varies by institution)

Program Focus

  • Cardiac and pulmonary support during surgery
  • Technology and equipment used in cardiovascular care
  • Emergency procedures and blood management

Curriculum & Skills

  • Cardiovascular physiology and pathology
  • Operating heart-lung machines
  • Blood circulation management
  • Emergency care protocols
  • Patient monitoring and vital signs tracking

Key Responsibilities

  • Operate and monitor heart-lung machines during surgery
  • Ensure safe circulation and oxygenation of blood
  • Collaborate with surgeons and healthcare teams
  • Manage equipment and respond to emergencies

Career Opportunities

  • Cardiac Perfusionist
  • Clinical Perfusionist
  • Perfusion Technologist
  • Educator or Trainer in Perfusion Technology
  • R&D Roles in Medical Equipment and Technologies

Top Colleges Offering This Course

  • JIPMER
  • AIIMS Rishikesh
  • Sanjay Gandhi Postgraduate Institute of Medical Sciences
  • St. John's National Academy of Health Sciences

Salary & Scope

  • Entry-Level: ₹2 – ₹5 LPA
  • Mid-Level: ₹6 – ₹9 LPA
  • Senior-Level: ₹10 – ₹15+ LPA
  • Global Demand: Strong job prospects in USA, Canada, and Europe

Factors Affecting Salary

  • Experience: More experienced perfusionists command higher pay
  • Location: Metropolitan areas often offer better packages
  • Institution: Private hospitals usually offer more than public hospitals
  • Specialization: Advanced skills like ECMO can boost earnings

In summary, a B.Sc. in Perfusion Technology opens the door to a highly specialized and rewarding career in cardiovascular healthcare. With growing demand and excellent salary potential both in India and abroad, it's a great choice for those passionate about medical technology and patient care.
