BNYS - Naturopathy and Yogic Sciences Career Path

The Bachelor of Naturopathy and Yogic Sciences (BNYS) is a 5.5-year undergraduate degree program that integrates natural healing techniques and yogic practices. It includes 4.5 years of academic study followed by a 1-year internship, preparing students for careers in holistic health and wellness.

What is BNYS?

BNYS combines naturopathy—which emphasizes self-healing through natural therapies—and yogic sciences, such as asanas, meditation, and breathing techniques. This approach focuses on treating the root cause of illness and promoting long-term wellness.

Program Duration

  • Total Duration: 5.5 years
  • Academic Study: 4.5 years
  • Internship: 1 year of compulsory clinical training

Eligibility & Admission

  • 10+2 or equivalent with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology
  • Minimum 50% aggregate in PCB subjects
  • Some institutes may conduct entrance tests or rely on NEET scores

Course Curriculum

  • Principles of Naturopathy and Yoga
  • Human Anatomy & Physiology
  • Nutrition, Biochemistry, Pathology
  • Yoga Therapy, Hydrotherapy, Massage Therapy
  • Acupuncture, Fasting Therapy, Lifestyle Medicine
  • Psychology, Community Medicine, and Counseling

Why Choose BNYS?

  • Focus on non-invasive, drug-free natural healing
  • Emphasis on preventive care and overall wellness
  • Opportunities in a growing wellness and fitness industry
  • Aligned with increasing demand for alternative medicine

Career Opportunities

  • Private Clinics: Start or join naturopathy/yoga centers
  • Wellness & Spa Resorts: Work as yoga or naturopathy consultant
  • Hospitals & Healthcare Centers: Join integrated treatment teams
  • Research Institutions: Assist or lead studies in natural healing
  • Government Health Programs: Work in AYUSH departments
  • Lifestyle & Corporate Wellness: Serve as a lifestyle consultant

Salary Range

  • Entry-Level: ₹2 LPA – ₹3.5 LPA
  • Mid-Level: ₹4 LPA – ₹6 LPA
  • Experienced Professionals: ₹7 LPA+ depending on specialization

Salaries are influenced by experience, location, sector (private/public), and individual practice growth.

Postgraduate Options

  • MD in Yoga/Naturopathy
  • MBA in Hospital/Health Management
  • MPH (Public Health)
  • MSc in Clinical Psychology or Health Sciences
  • PhD in Yoga, Natural Medicine, or related fields

Top Institutions Offering BNYS

  • Morarji Desai National Institute of Yoga, New Delhi
  • JSS Institute of Naturopathy, Coimbatore
  • Gandhiji Nature Cure College, Hyderabad
