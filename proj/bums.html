BUMS - Unani Medicine Career Path

A BUMS (Bachelor of Unani Medicine and Surgery) is a 5.5-year undergraduate degree rooted in traditional Unani healing. It blends ancient Greek, Arabic, and Indian medical systems and emphasizes natural healing through herbal remedies, dietary changes, and holistic care.

Course Details

  • Focus: Unani principles, diagnosis, herbal remedies, surgery
  • Duration: 5.5 years (including 1-year internship)
  • Eligibility: 10+2 (PCB) + NEET qualification

Career Paths

  • Unani Doctor/Hakim: Practice in clinics, hospitals, or independently
  • Research Scientist: Focus on Unani herbal research and innovation
  • Unani Pharmacist: Preparation and distribution of Unani medicines
  • Unani Therapist: Treatment through Unani therapy techniques
  • Lecturer/Professor: Teaching in Unani medical colleges

Salary Range

  • Unani Therapist: ₹2.5 LPA
  • Hakeem (Doctor): ₹3.5 LPA
  • Lecturer: ₹4.5 LPA
  • Consultant: ₹3 LPA
  • Pharmacist: ₹4 LPA
  • Chemist: ₹6.5 LPA

Scope

The Unani system is gaining recognition both in India and globally for its natural healing methods. Graduates can work in healthcare, pharmaceutical companies, wellness centers, or pursue research in herbal therapies. There's growing integration of Unani medicine with modern healthcare.

Regulatory Body

The course is regulated by the Central Council of Indian Medicine (CCIM) to maintain education and practice standards.

Curriculum Highlights

  • Study of bodily humors and natural healing
  • Herbal pharmacology and treatments
  • Unani surgical and therapeutic practices
  • Lifestyle and diet-based treatments

Admission Requirements

  • NEET Qualification
  • Class 12 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • Minimum age and marks as per institutional norms

Job Prospects

BUMS graduates can treat chronic diseases using traditional Unani methods, work in government and private hospitals, open their own clinics, or contribute to research in integrated medical systems.
