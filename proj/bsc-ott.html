B.Sc. in Operation Theatre Technology (OTT)

A B.Sc. in Operation Theatre Technology (OTT) is a 3-year undergraduate program that prepares individuals to support surgical teams and manage the technical operations of operating rooms. It equips students with the skills needed to assist in surgeries, maintain surgical equipment, and manage patient care before and after operations.

Key Highlights

  • Duration: 3 years
  • Eligibility: 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology (minimum 50%)

Program Focus

  • Assisting surgeons and anesthesiologists
  • Managing and sterilizing the operating room
  • Patient monitoring and safety during procedures

Curriculum & Skills

  • Subjects: Surgical instrumentation, anesthesia, infection control, and patient care
  • Hands-on practical training in hospitals and clinical environments
  • Skills in sterilization, technical equipment handling, communication, and teamwork

Key Responsibilities

  • Prepare and maintain OT equipment and surgical tools
  • Assist during surgeries and monitor patients
  • Maintain OT hygiene and manage anesthesia supplies
  • Ensure proper sterilization and infection control

Career Opportunities

  • Operation Theatre Technician
  • Anesthesia Technician
  • Surgical Assistant
  • Lab Technician

Areas of Employment

  • Hospitals (public & private)
  • Multi-specialty and specialty clinics
  • Medical research institutions
  • Opportunities abroad in healthcare facilities

Salary Structure

  • Entry-Level: ₹1.2 – ₹1.4 LPA
  • Mid-Level: ₹1.5 – ₹1.7 LPA
  • Senior-Level: ₹2.4 LPA and above
  • Maximum Potential: Up to ₹20 LPA (with experience & specialization)

Career Growth & Further Education

  • Opportunities to become OT supervisors or department heads
  • Further studies such as Master's in OTT, hospital management, or specialized certifications
  • International certification can open global career options

In conclusion, a B.Sc. in Operation Theatre Technology is a vital course in the medical field that opens doors to rewarding careers in surgery support and healthcare services, both in India and globally.
