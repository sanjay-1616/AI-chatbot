BHMS - Homeopathy Career Path

A BHMS (Bachelor of Homeopathic Medicine and Surgery) degree offers a career in alternative medicine through homeopathy—focusing on the principle of "like cures like." BHMS graduates use diluted natural substances to trigger the body’s healing process.

Course Details

  • Focus: Homeopathic diagnosis and treatment with natural remedies
  • Duration: 5.5 years (4.5 years of academics + 1 year internship)
  • Eligibility: 10+2 (PCB) + NEET qualification
  • Regulatory Body: National Commission for Homeopathy (NCH)

Curriculum Highlights

  • Homeopathic Materia Medica
  • Organon of Medicine & Philosophy
  • Anatomy, Physiology, and Pathology
  • Pharmacy and Repertory
  • Clinical training and diagnosis methods

Career Paths After BHMS

  • Homeopathic Doctor: Clinical practice and patient consultation
  • Private Practitioner: Establish own clinic or holistic health center
  • Researcher: Explore homeopathic drug efficacy and new treatments
  • Lecturer/Professor: Teach in homeopathic medical colleges
  • Hospital Roles: Work in private/public hospitals, wellness centers
  • Spa & Resort Roles: Holistic care in luxury wellness facilities

Salary Expectations

  • Entry-Level: ₹2.5 – ₹6 LPA
  • Mid-Level: ₹6 – ₹12 LPA
  • Senior-Level: ₹12 – ₹25 LPA

Factors affecting salary: location, years of experience, type of employment (clinic/hospital), and specialization.

Scope and Demand

Homeopathy is gaining global attention for being non-invasive and cost-effective. Many prefer natural treatments with fewer side effects. BHMS graduates are in demand in India and abroad across wellness, research, and clinical care.

Postgraduate Options

  • MD (Homeopathy)
  • PG Diploma in Clinical Research or Healthcare Management
  • MBA in Hospital Administration
