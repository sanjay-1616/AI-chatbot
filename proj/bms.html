BMS – Bachelor of Management Studies & Battery Management System

The abbreviation BMS can refer to two very different domains:

  • Bachelor of Management Studies – an undergraduate business degree
  • Battery Management System – a technology used in battery-powered systems like EVs

Battery Management System (BMS)

A Battery Management System is a technology used to ensure the safe and efficient operation of rechargeable batteries, especially in applications like electric vehicles (EVs), drones, and UPS systems.

Key Functions:

  • Monitors voltage, current, and temperature of battery cells
  • Prevents overcharging, over-discharging, and overheating
  • Ensures battery health and prolongs lifespan
  • Balances cells to maintain performance

Technologies Used:

  • Bluetooth & UART for connectivity
  • Microcontrollers for processing data
  • Embedded software for control algorithms

Applications:

  • Electric Vehicles (EVs)
  • Consumer electronics (laptops, smartphones)
  • Solar energy storage systems
  • Uninterruptible Power Supplies (UPS)

Importance:

  • Critical for safety and reliability in EVs
  • Improves energy efficiency and lifespan
  • Supports real-time monitoring and diagnostics
