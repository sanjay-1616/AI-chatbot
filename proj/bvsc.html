BVSc & AH - Veterinary Science Career Path

The Bachelor of Veterinary Science and Animal Husbandry (BVSc & AH) is a 5-year undergraduate medical degree that equips students to diagnose, manage, and treat animal diseases while promoting animal health and welfare. It includes training in animal anatomy, physiology, pharmacology, pathology, surgery, and preventive medicine.

Program Overview

  • Duration: 5 years (including internship)
  • Focus: Animal care, veterinary medicine, surgery, and animal husbandry
  • Eligibility: 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology

Curriculum Highlights

  • Animal Anatomy and Physiology
  • Pathology and Microbiology
  • Pharmacology and Toxicology
  • Surgery and Radiology
  • Animal Genetics and Breeding
  • Veterinary Public Health
  • Preventive Medicine

Career Opportunities

  • Veterinary Doctor: Clinical practice in hospitals and private clinics
  • Veterinary Surgeon: Performing surgical procedures on animals
  • Animal Breeders: Working in breeding and livestock management programs
  • Research Scientist: Animal health research and drug development
  • Veterinary Officers: Working in public health and government animal care departments
  • Junior Veterinary Doctor: Supporting senior doctors and gaining experience
  • Other: Animal welfare organizations, food industry, pharma companies

Further Studies

  • MVSc – Master of Veterinary Science (Specializations like surgery, pathology, etc.)
  • PhD – Research in veterinary fields

Salary Expectations

  • Entry-Level: ₹30,000 – ₹50,000/month
  • Mid-Level: ₹4 – ₹6 LPA
  • Specialized Roles: ₹6 – ₹7+ LPA with experience

Factors Affecting Salary

  • Experience: More experienced vets earn higher
  • Location: Salaries vary based on region and demand
  • Specialization: Surgeons and researchers may earn significantly more
  • Employer Type: Private clinics, government jobs, and pharma firms differ in pay scale

Top Veterinary Colleges in India

  • College of Veterinary and Animal Sciences, Bikaner
  • Madras Veterinary College, Chennai
  • Bombay Veterinary College, Mumbai
