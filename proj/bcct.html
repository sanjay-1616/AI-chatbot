B.Com (Travel and Tourism)

A B.Com in Travel and Tourism is a 3-year undergraduate program combining business fundamentals with specialized training in the travel and hospitality industry. It prepares students for careers in tourism, hospitality, finance, and customer service.

Course Focus

  • Emphasizes analytical, strategic, and policy-making skills within the tourism sector
  • Develops knowledge in tourism history, economics, environmental impact, and industry operations

Curriculum Highlights

  • Tourism and Hospitality Management
  • Travel Agency Operations
  • Airline and Hotel Management
  • Tourism Marketing
  • Financial Management
  • Field visits, internships, and case studies for practical exposure
  • Communication skills and customer service training

Career Opportunities

  • Travel Consultant: Planning and coordinating travel itineraries
  • Tour Organizer: Designing tours and holiday packages
  • Hotel Manager: Managing hotel or resort operations
  • Event Planner: Organizing travel-related conferences or events
  • Airlines: Ground staff, flight operations, customer service
  • Tourism Department: Working in government travel and tourism boards

Higher Studies Options

  • Master’s in Tourism Management
  • MBA in Travel & Hospitality
  • Postgraduate Diploma in Event or Hotel Management
  • Financial Certifications (CA, CMA, etc.) for business-focused roles

Why Choose This Course?

  • Growing Industry: Huge demand for skilled professionals in tourism and hospitality
  • Diverse Career Options: Entry to senior roles across multiple sectors
  • Business Skills: Strong foundation in finance, operations, and management

Salary & Scope

  • Entry-Level: INR 3–6 LPA (e.g., Travel Agent, Tour Guide)
  • Mid-Level: INR 5–10 LPA (e.g., Event Planner, Hotel Supervisor)
  • Senior-Level: INR 8–15 LPA (e.g., Resort Manager, Operations Head)
  • Specialized Roles: MICE Planners or Destination Managers – up to INR 20 LPA

Transferable Skills from B.Com

  • Accounting and Finance Knowledge
  • Business Administration Skills
  • Strategic Thinking and Policy Development
  • Communication and Customer Service Expertise

B.Com in Travel and Tourism is an ideal program for students who wish to combine business acumen with a passion for travel and hospitality, unlocking opportunities in one of the world's most dynamic industries.
