Bachelor of Accounting and Finance (BAF) Career Path

BAF is a three-year undergraduate degree focusing on accounting and finance. It offers students comprehensive knowledge in financial concepts, accounting principles, taxation, investment analysis, and risk management.

Salary Packages

  • Entry-Level: ₹2.5–4 LPA (Accounting Assistant, Tax Assistant)
  • Mid-Level: ₹5–8 LPA (Financial Analyst, Auditor, Finance Manager)
  • Senior-Level: ₹10–14 LPA+ (Chartered Accountant, CFO)
  • High-End: Up to ₹55 LPA for CAs with 5+ years experience
  • Factors: Certification (CA, ACCA, CFA), experience, location, company

Scope & Career Opportunities

  • Jobs in banking, insurance, corporate finance, and investment firms
  • Roles: Tax Consultant, Financial Analyst, Auditor, Risk Manager
  • High demand in both public and private sectors
  • Opportunities in global financial institutions and MNCs

Top Recruiters

  • Deloitte
  • KPMG
  • EY (Ernst & Young)
  • PWC
  • ICICI Bank, HDFC Bank
  • J.P. Morgan, Goldman Sachs

Higher Education Options

  • Chartered Accountancy (CA)
  • Certified Public Accountant (CPA)
  • Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA)
  • MBA in Finance or Banking

What is BAF?

BAF is a degree designed for students interested in accounting and finance careers. It covers both theory and practical applications in financial analysis, taxation, auditing, and portfolio management.

BAF Curriculum

  • Accounting: Financial Accounting, Cost Accounting, Auditing
  • Finance: Investment Analysis, Financial Markets, Risk Management
  • Business: Business Law, Economics, Business Communication

Career Path

  • Start as Accounting Assistant or Analyst
  • Move to roles like Tax Consultant, Financial Planner, or Auditor
  • Advance to leadership roles like Finance Manager or CFO

Technological Advancements

Finance careers now require familiarity with data analytics, fintech tools, ERP systems like SAP, and AI-driven accounting platforms.

Duration

The BAF degree typically takes 3 years to complete.
