Bachelor of Financial Markets (BFM)

BFM stands for Bachelor of Financial Markets, a 3-year undergraduate program designed to equip students with in-depth knowledge of financial markets, investments, and risk management. It prepares students for careers in the dynamic and growing financial services industry.

Program Overview

  • Focus: Stock trading, derivatives, investment analysis, and risk management
  • Duration: 3 years
  • Learning Approach: Theoretical concepts, case studies, projects, and industrial visits

Curriculum Highlights

  • Financial Accounting
  • Business Statistics
  • Security Analysis and Portfolio Management
  • Corporate Finance
  • Risk Management
  • Derivatives Markets
  • Investment Banking

Career Opportunities

  • Investment Banker
  • Stock Broker
  • Financial Analyst
  • Portfolio Manager
  • Risk Manager
  • Corporate Treasurer

Why Choose BFM?

  • Growing Field: Expanding job market in financial sectors globally
  • Practical Learning: Real-world exposure through case studies and industry projects
  • Comprehensive Knowledge: Covers wide range of finance concepts and tools
