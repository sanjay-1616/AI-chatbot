Automation & Robotics Career Path

Automation and Robotics are closely related fields that focus on automating tasks and developing robots to perform various functions autonomously or semi-autonomously. Automation refers to using technology to perform tasks with minimal human intervention, while robotics involves creating robots that can carry out tasks in specific industries.

Salary Packages

  • Fresh Graduates: INR 4-10 LPA (Depends on role, company, and skills)
  • Experienced Professionals: INR 8-10 LPA (2+ years of experience)
  • Specialized Roles: INR 14-20 LPA (Robotics Consultant), INR 10-16 LPA (Drone Engineer)

Scope & Career Opportunities

  • Growing demand for skilled professionals in automation and robotics
  • Diverse applications: manufacturing, healthcare, space research, entertainment, etc.
  • Opportunities in research and development of new robotic systems
  • Roles include Automation Engineer, Robotics Engineer, Robotics Consultant, Drone Engineer

What is Automation & Robotics?

Automation is the use of technology to perform tasks with minimal human input. Robotics is the branch of engineering focused on the creation and operation of robots designed to carry out various tasks autonomously or with minimal human intervention.

Robotics & Automation Curriculum

  • Subjects: Mechanical Design, Electronics, Control Systems, AI, Machine Learning, Software Development
  • Hands-on experience with robotic systems, CAD software, and control algorithms

Career Path

  • Start as an Automation Engineer or Robotics Engineer and progress to specialized roles
  • Work in R&D, system integration, or robotic process automation
  • Opportunities for leadership in robotics-based industries or tech companies

Technological Advancements

The field is advancing with new innovations in autonomous vehicles, industrial robots, AI, and machine learning, increasing the demand for professionals in robotics and automation.

Duration

The undergraduate course in Automation & Robotics typically lasts 4 years, followed by potential postgraduate education.
