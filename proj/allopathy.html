MBBS - Allopathy Career Path

An MBBS in Allopathy (modern medicine) offers a career path with competitive salaries and broad scope for specialization. Entry-level doctors earn ₹6–7.2 LPA, while senior specialists can make ₹12–24 LPA.

Salary Packages

  • Entry-Level: ₹6–7.2 LPA (General Physician, Junior Medical Officer, Medical Assistant)
  • Mid-Level: ₹7.2–10 LPA (Medical Officer, Pediatrician)
  • Senior-Level: ₹12–24 LPA (Consultant, Surgeon, Hospital Director)
  • Factors: Experience, specialization, sector (private/govt), location
  • Benefits: Bonuses, health insurance, housing, flexible hours

Scope & Career Opportunities

  • Work as general practitioners or specialists
  • Employment in hospitals, clinics, military, and independent practices
  • Specializations: Cardiology, Neurology, Surgery, Pediatrics, etc.
  • R&D roles in pharma companies and research institutes
  • Opportunities in healthcare management & consulting

Higher Education Options

What is Allopathic Medicine?

Allopathy, or modern medicine, is evidence-based and uses clinical methods, surgery, and drugs to diagnose and treat diseases.

MBBS Curriculum

  • Subjects: Anatomy, Physiology, Biochemistry, Pathology, Pharmacology, Surgery, Medicine, Pediatrics
  • Hands-on clinical training in hospitals

Career Path

  • General practice or choose a specialization
  • Opportunities in teaching, research, and hospital administration

Scientific Approach

Allopathy uses scientific research and clinical trials to develop effective treatments.

Duration

The MBBS course typically lasts 5.5 years (including 1-year internship).
